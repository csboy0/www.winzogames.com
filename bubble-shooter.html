Rules of Bubble Shooter Game?

01

It's important to remember that this is a points-based game. Because the Bubble Shooter game is a points-based game, you should concentrate on the points being added to your score. The game starts with a slew of bubbles strewn over the screen.

02

When employed appropriately, the Bubble Shooter online game's powers ups have a specific goal to complete. The Bomb is the second power up, and it is intended to blow out bubbles in a cluster, just like its name suggests. 03

04

Tips and Tricks for Bubble Shooter Game

Have a Strategy

As you have an option of switching between bubbles of two colors, set a strategy and launch your hits accordingly.

Use Your Powerups

Most powerups help you massively as a player. Those powerups are, fireball (bursts one complete line), bomb (bursts bubbles in a group), and wild bubble (bursts all the bubbles of one color).

Choose the Target

Always choose the bubble that is incorporated in larger groups. You will get more points for popping a larger group of bubbles.

Take Strategic Shots

Take the advantage of walls to hit the bubbles placed at the higher end.

Analyze the Board

Initially, carefully check the board and set a strategy to win the game. Make sure that you hit the bubbles only according to your set plan.

Recheck Before Hitting

Reaching out the target is one of the most important things in this game and recheck your aim while hitting the bubble.
